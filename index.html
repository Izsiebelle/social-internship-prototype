Why should you do an internship?

Build Your Network

Throughout the 8 weeks you will connect with local entrepreneurs and organizations within the topic of your research. You will meet and work alongside teens

Gain a Mentor

Be paired with a entrepreneur mentor during the very first week of the challenge to help coach you and your teammates.

Make an Impact

Work within a small group with your peers to solve real-problems in the community.

8 week Internship Breakdown

Week 1
Onboarding

Team bonding, meet your mentor, tool training and other onboarding fun.

Week 2
Define the problem

Students define the problem that they will be targeting through out their internship. Dive into the beginings of design thinking.

Week 3
Intro to human centered design

Emphathize adn define the needs of the users and gain a clearer understanding of the problem. Talk to users and identify potential partners and key stakeholders.

Week 4
Ideate and Prototype

Students begin to come up with solutions and delve into creating a prototype to present to potential users.

Week 5
Test and Interate

Students test their ideas by engaging potential users. Students identify percieved value of their ideas.

Week 6
Launch Week

Students launch their ideas to test user engaement and expereince.

Week 7
Launch week pt. 2 and pitch preperation

Students adjust idea from week 1's launch findings.

Week 8
Pitch Week

Students pitch to key stakeholders, guest judges and others invited.
